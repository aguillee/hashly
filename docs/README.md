# Welcome to Hashly

**Hashly** is the Hedera ecosystem hub — a platform where the community discovers, votes on, and ranks everything happening on Hedera Hashgraph.

## What You Can Do

- **Discover events** — Browse upcoming NFT mints, meetups, hackathons, DreamCast pools, and forever mints in a unified calendar.
- **Vote on-chain** — Every vote is recorded on the Hedera Consensus Service (HCS) for full transparency. Hold Dragon or El Santuario NFTs to boost your voting power.
- **Explore the ecosystem** — Find and rank Hedera projects, tokens, and NFT collections.
- **Earn XP & compete** — Complete daily and weekly missions, climb the seasonal leaderboard, and win prizes.
- **Map the community** — Pin yourself on the HashWorld 3D globe and discover Hedera builders worldwide.
- **Check NFT rarity** — Instantly check rarity rankings for Santuario Hedera Dragon NFTs.

## Why Hashly?

Hedera has a growing ecosystem but no central place to discover what's happening. Hashly fills that gap by giving visibility to projects, events, and creators — with rankings decided by the community, not algorithms.

All votes are recorded on-chain via HCS, making Hashly one of the few platforms where community rankings are verifiable and transparent.
