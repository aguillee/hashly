# HashWorld Globe

HashWorld is an interactive 3D globe that maps the Hedera community worldwide. Find builders, projects, and community members — and pin yourself on the map.

## How It Works

HashWorld displays community members and projects as pins on a WebGL-rendered 3D globe. Each pin represents an approved profile and links to their X (Twitter) account.

## Profile Types

| Type | Description |
|---|---|
| User | Individual community member |
| Builder | Developer or technical contributor |
| Person | Other community participant |
| Project | Ecosystem project (auto-created on approval) |

## Creating Your Profile

1. Connect your wallet.
2. Go to **HashWorld** from the sidebar.
3. Click **Join the Map**.
4. Fill in your profile:
   - **Display name** (required)
   - **Type** — User, Builder, or Person
   - **Country** (required) — determines your pin location
   - **Twitter/X handle** (optional)
   - **Bio** (optional)
   - **Avatar** (image upload)
5. Submit for review.

### Approval

Profiles are reviewed by admins before appearing on the globe. This prevents spam and ensures quality.

## Mission Reward

Creating a HashWorld profile is one of the highest-value missions on Hashly:

**HashWorld Citizen** — 1,500 XP (one-time, permanent)

This makes it one of the most rewarding single actions you can take on the platform.

## Project Pins

When an ecosystem project is approved, it automatically gets a pin on the HashWorld globe based on the country selected during application. This means the ecosystem directory and HashWorld are interconnected.

## Interacting with the Globe

- **Rotate** — Click and drag to spin the globe.
- **Zoom** — Scroll to zoom in/out.
- **Click pins** — See profile details and link to their X account.
- **Search** — Find specific community members.
