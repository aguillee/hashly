# Referral Program

Grow the Hashly community and earn passive XP through the referral system.

## How It Works

1. **Find your referral code** — It's automatically generated when you create your account. Check your profile page.
2. **Share it** — Give your code to friends or share it on social media.
3. **They apply it** — New users enter your referral code when they join.
4. **You earn commission** — Get 5% of all XP your referred users earn, forever.

## Commission Details

- **Rate**: 5% of every XP award your referrals earn.
- **Scope**: Applies to mission claims, badge points, and all other XP sources.
- **Duration**: Permanent — as long as they're active, you earn.
- **Impact on referral**: Your commission doesn't reduce their reward. They get 100%, you get an extra 5%.

## Referral Missions

| Mission | Requirement | XP |
|---|---|---|
| Referral x1 | 1 activated referral | 100 |
| Referral x3 | 3 activated referrals | 500 |

These are seasonal missions — you can claim them once per season.

## Strategy

- Share your code in Hedera community channels.
- Refer active users — the more they participate, the more you earn.
- Combined with daily missions, referrals create a compounding XP engine.
