# Attendance Badges

Attendance badges are NFTs minted to attendees of Hedera ecosystem meetups and events. They serve as proof of attendance and contribute to your seasonal leaderboard points.

## How It Works

### For Attendees

1. **Attend a meetup** that has an attendance badge enabled.
2. **Scan the QR code** displayed at the event (or shared by the host).
3. **Check in** — Enter the time-based code from the QR to confirm your attendance.
4. **Receive your badge** — The host airdrops the attendance badge NFT to your wallet.
5. **Earn badge points** — Owning badges contributes to your seasonal leaderboard score.

### Check-in Process

The check-in code is a 12-character time-based code that changes periodically. This ensures only people present at the event can check in. Each check-in is recorded on-chain via HCS for transparency.

## Badge Points & Missions

Attendance badges count toward your seasonal points and unlock missions:

| Mission | Requirement | XP |
|---|---|---|
| Badge x1 | Own 1 attendance badge | 300 |
| Badge x3 | Own 3 attendance badges | 1,000 |

Badge ownership is verified on-chain — badges must be in your connected wallet to count.

## Badge Lifecycle

Badges go through several stages:

1. **Draft** — Host creates the badge (name, description, image).
2. **Token Created** — NFT token is created on Hedera.
3. **Minted** — Individual badge NFTs are minted.
4. **Distributed** — Badges are airdropped to attendee wallets.
5. **Expired** — Distribution window closes after deadline (up to 3 airdrop attempts).
