# ❓ What is Hashly?

**Hashly** is a community-driven discovery platform for the **Hedera Hashgraph** ecosystem. It brings together NFT mints, meetups, hackathons, tokens, and projects into a single hub where users can **explore, vote, and rank** everything happening on Hedera.

---

## 🎯 Our Mission

> Give **visibility to every project and event** being built on Hedera — with rankings decided **transparently** by the community through on-chain voting.

---

## 🏛️ Key Principles

### 🗳️ Community-Driven Rankings

Unlike centralized platforms where visibility depends on paid promotion, **Hashly rankings** are generated by votes from real Hedera wallet holders. Every vote is recorded on **Hedera Consensus Service (HCS)**, making the process **fully transparent and verifiable**.

### 💎 NFT-Powered Governance

Holders of specific Hedera NFTs receive **increased voting power**:

| NFT Collection | Token ID | Boost |
|---|---|---|
| 🐉 Santuario Hedera Dragons | `0.0.7235629` | **+1 vote** per NFT |
| 🏛️ El Santuario | `0.0.9954622` | **+5 votes** (one-time) |

This means active community members who own ecosystem NFTs have **a stronger voice** in the rankings.

### ⭐ Gamified Participation

Hashly rewards participation through:

- 📋 **Daily and weekly missions** that grant XP
- 🏆 **Seasonal leaderboards** with prize pools that vary each season
- 🎖️ **Attendance badges** for participating in in-person meetups
- 🤝 **Referral bonuses** for growing the community

### ⛓️ On-Chain Transparency

All votes are submitted to **Hedera Consensus Service (HCS)** topics, creating an **immutable** record. Anyone can verify the authenticity of votes by reading the HCS topic messages directly on the Hedera network.

---

## 🚫 What Hashly Is NOT

- **Not an exchange** — Hashly does not facilitate trading, swaps, or financial transactions.
- **Not financial advice** — Rankings reflect community sentiment, not investment recommendations.
- **Not a minting platform** — Events link to the original project's mint site.

---

## 👥 Who Built Hashly?

Hashly was created by a team of **Hedera ecosystem enthusiasts** who saw the need for a central hub connecting community members with the projects they care about. 💜
